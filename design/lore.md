You were an alchemist who was seeking the Magnum Opus. In your searches you built many safety and countermeasures due to your cautious nature, the strongest of which you hoped to never have to use. One day, as you neared completion on your philosopher stone, you were attacked by something. Activating your greatest failsafe, you sent yourself back in time / to a parallel world where you lost most of your memories but have a drive to discover alchemy, the secrets of the mine you spent your life at, and the scattered notes within your ruined laboratory. Each puzzle and piece of information is locked behind increasingly difficult alchemical puzzles, ensuring that you don't access secrets you're not ready for while preventing outsiders from gaining access.
